## The Problem

AI broke hiring.

Not intentionally — but completely.

Candidates use AI to write resumes. Every resume sounds the same. Hiring managers are buried under thousands of applications they cannot meaningfully distinguish. Good candidates get passed over. Bad hires still happen. The signal is gone.

On the candidate side, the resume was already an imperfect tool. A flat document trying to represent a three-dimensional human being. AI made it worse. Now everyone sounds polished, everyone has the right keywords, and nobody stands out.

On the employer side, the volume is suffocating. LinkedIn alone sees 11,000 applications submitted every minute. Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) filter the pile down — but they filter by keywords, not by humans. The best candidate in the stack might never get seen.

The resume is dead. Nobody has replaced it yet.

**We are.**

## The Solution

A multi-format candidate narrative platform that lets job seekers tell their own story — in audio, video, visuals, and text — and gives hiring managers a smarter, faster, more human way to find the right person.

One shareable link. Every version of you.

## How It Works

### The Candidate Experience

1. **Upload** -- Resume and LinkedIn profile
2. **Context form** -- Target role, key wins, tone, career highlights
3. **Platform generates** -- An elite AI prompt engineered specifically for their background
4. **NotebookLM produces** -- A full suite of professional career assets
5. **Candidate curates** -- Chooses which assets appear on their profile
6. **One link** -- Shareable anywhere: email signature, LinkedIn, Indeed, job applications

### The Assets Candidates Receive

| Asset | Format | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Audio Overview | 2-3 min podcast narrative | Commuters, engaged reviewers |
| Video Overview | 90 sec cinematic career story | Visual consumers |
| Slide Deck | Structured career presentation | Detail-oriented reviewers |
| Career Infographic | Visual timeline and key stats | Skimmers, social sharing |
| AI Bullet Summary | 5-7 key highlights | 10-second first impression |
| ATS Resume | Clean formatted text version | Hiring systems |

### The Employer Experience

When a hiring manager clicks a candidate's link, a pop-up modal opens — no new tab, no navigation away. Inside the modal:

- **AI bullet summary** at the top for instant context
- **Media tabs** — audio, video, slide deck, infographic
- **Full resume** available for download
- **Connect button** for direct outreach
- **Save to playlist** for shortlisting

### The Playlist

Hiring managers build candidate queues — like Spotify but for people. They can:

- Play through audio narratives back to back
- Like, pass, or tag each candidate
- Add private notes
- Share playlists with their team
- Track status (Screening / Interview / Offer / Passed)

### The Feedback Loop

Candidates see when their profile was viewed, how long the employer engaged, and can receive structured or open-ended feedback. This closes a loop that has never existed before in hiring — candidates finally know why they were or were not moved forward.
